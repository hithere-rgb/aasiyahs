History

Connect Four is a classic two-player strategy game invented by Howard Wexler in

1974. Originally named "The Vertical Four," it was later marketed by Milton Bradley

under the name Connect Four.The game involves players taking turns dropping

colored discs into a vertical grid with the goal of forming a line of four discs

horizontally, vertically, or diagonally before their opponent can do the same.Connect

Four quickly gained popularity and became a staple in many households, schools,

and tournaments worldwide.Its simple rules and engaging gameplay have made it a

timeless favorite for players of all ages.
